MAICOBI Twist Pins

Elevate your home decor with these versatile bed skirt pins. Crafted from a combination of high-quality linen fabric and 304 stainless steel needles, they are built to last and offer a secure fit without causing any damage to your fabrics.

Featuring a unique flax-covered twist mechanism, these pins ensure effortless installation and removal, making them ideal for use on bed skirts, sofa covers, and upholstery overlays. The beaded tip design is engineered to navigate through multiple fabric layers smoothly, maintaining your furniture's neat appearance and prolonging its lifespan.

Designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind, MAICOBI twist pins are non-toxic and environmentally friendly, ensuring safety in your living space. Whether you're renovating your bedroom or managing everyday furniture maintenance, these pins are the perfect addition to your DIY toolkit.

  • Beaded tip design allows easy insertion into multiple fabric layers
