Stylish & Durable Bed Risers for Enhanced Comfort and Convenience

These ANNKUT bed risers are the ideal choice for anyone looking to elevate their furniture safely and efficiently. With a total height of 6 inches and an actual lift of 5.5 inches, they create additional space under the bed, making it easier to store and access your belongings. Whether you need a little extra room or want to improve your sleep setup, these risers offer a practical and effective solution.

Engineered for strength and stability, they feature a robust heavy-duty build that can support up to 6000 lbs. The built-in rubber pads on the bottom ensure a secure grip on your floor, preventing slipping or wobbling for a smooth and hassle-free experience. Their sturdy 1-inch internal frame adds critical support, making them reliable for long-term use.

Designed with versatility in mind, these bed risers are suitable for a wide range of furniture, including beds, desks, tables, and sofas. They are especially beneficial for individuals with mobility challenges or back pain, offering improved accessibility and comfort when transitioning from sitting to standing.
