Beautyrest 16-inch Full Air Mattress

Looking for a reliable and comfortable sleep solution for any occasion? The Beautyrest 16-inch Full Air Mattress is designed to deliver exceptional support and durability for both temporary use and regular sleep. Engineered with a coil-in-coil beam system, it mimics the feel of a traditional mattress while providing added cushioning and a smooth sleep surface.

The air mattress features raised edges that enhance stability and help maintain its shape throughout the night, ensuring a secure sleeping experience. Its puncture-resistant vinyl construction is built tough to prevent leaks and damage, making it ideal for outdoor adventures, camping trips, or hosting overnight guests.

With a powerful electric Express Pump included, inflating and deflating the mattress is quick and easy. Plus, the compact design and built-in storage bag make it simple to transport and store when not in use. This product is proudly made in the U.S. and comes with a one-year warranty, giving you confidence in your purchase.
