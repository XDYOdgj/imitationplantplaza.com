8 Self Adhesive Furniture Bed Risers

Made from high-quality EVA material, these bed risers are built for long-lasting durability and resistance to corrosion. Each riser adds just 1/2 inch in height, giving you more space to clean under your furniture and enhancing the overall layout of your room. The self-adhesive backing makes installation quick and tool-free, so you can easily secure them to your furniture legs without causing any damage.

Whether you're looking to elevate a table, sofa, chair, bed, or cabinet, these risers are the perfect solution for optimizing your living or working space. They create additional room for storage and help keep your floors clean by preventing direct contact between furniture and the ground. Especially useful in homes with children or pets, these risers are easy to remove and reuse, making them a versatile and practical choice for any home.
