Sunifier Heavy Duty Bed Risers - 6-Inch L-Shaped 4-Pack

Elevate your furniture with the Sunifier 6-inch heavy duty bed risers, designed for effortless installation and long-lasting use. Made from high-quality PP material, they can safely support up to 5,000 pounds, ensuring your furniture stays stable and secure no matter how much weight it carries.

  • Designed for easy installation under furniture legs
  • Durable PP material built to handle heavy loads
  • Anti-slip TPU base protects floors from scratches and prevents shifting
  • Raises furniture height to create extra clearance for storage and cleaning
  • Semi-enclosed edge fits a range of furniture leg sizes for versatile use

Whether you're looking to improve your sleep experience by raising your bed or want to keep your floors clean with a robot vacuum cleaner, these bed risers are a smart solution for any home or office setup. Ideal for couches, tables, sofas, desks, and chairs, they offer practicality and comfort without compromising on quality.
